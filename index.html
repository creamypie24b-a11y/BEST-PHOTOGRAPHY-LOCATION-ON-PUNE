About Photography

Natural & Scenic Locations These spots offer greenery, water bodies, and a serene ambiance, often at a low or no entry cost. Mulshi Lake and Dam: Located about 40 km from Pune, Mulshi offers expansive views of the lake, lush hills, and open skies, ideal for dramatic landscape and nature photography. It is especially popular at sunrise or sunset. Empress Garden: A botanical garden in the Camp area, known for its well-maintained lawns, vibrant flowers, and tranquil pathways. It provides a peaceful, natural backdrop. Pu La Deshpande Garden: Also known as the Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden, this location features unique Japanese-style landscaping with bridges, water bodies, and manicured lawns, perfect for artistic and serene shoots. Khadakwasla Lake: The lake and surrounding hills provide a picturesque and calm setting. It is easily accessible and a budget-friendly option for various types of shoots. Vetal Tekdi (Arai Hill): This hill offers panoramic views of the city, making it a great spot for morning or evening shoots with natural light. Historic & Architectural Locations These locations add a regal, timeless, or dramatic feel to photos with their unique architecture. Aga Khan Palace: This majestic historical monument features grand architecture and expansive lawns, providing an elegant, formal, and vintage backdrop. Photography is generally allowed. Shaniwar Wada: A historic fortification in the heart of Pune, its grand gates and old stone walls create a dramatic setting for heritage or cultural-themed photos. A nominal entry fee is typically required. Pataleshwar Cave Temple: This ancient rock-cut temple provides a rugged and unique architectural backdrop, adding a touch of rustic charm to photos. Urban & Themed Locations For those seeking a more modern, trendy, or private setting. Koregaon Park: A trendy urban area known for its tree-lined avenues, chic cafes, and street art. It offers a vibrant mix of urban and natural elements suitable for lifestyle and fashion photography. Commercial Studios & Resorts: Several private studios and resorts in and around Pune (such as Cubic Studio or Pixocity in Surat) offer well-equipped spaces, specific backdrops, props, and lighting for a controlled environment. These typically require booking and involve a higher cost
